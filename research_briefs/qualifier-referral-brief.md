# Qualifier Referral Brief: Shelter in Toronto Tonight for a Youth

## Situation
A youth needs a shelter in Toronto tonight. Prioritize immediate, youth-specific emergency shelter and central intake pathways.

## Immediate referral path
1. **City of Toronto Central Intake / Shelter Referral**
   - **416-338-4766**
   - **1-877-338-3398**
   - **311**
   - Use for referral and bed access coordination tonight.

2. **211 Ontario / 211 Central**
   - **Dial 211**
   - Ask for **youth emergency shelter in Toronto tonight** and nearest available intake option.

3. **If the youth is in immediate danger**
   - **Call 911**

## Youth shelter options to mention
### Covenant House Toronto
- Serves youth **ages 16–24** in crisis / homelessness.
- 211 Central lists an **emergency shelter intake centre** with food, clothing, medical care, assessment, counselling, referrals, housing help, education and job supports.
- 211 notes **capacity 96** plus **2 emergency crisis beds** for survivors of human trafficking and sexual exploitation.
- Website: https://covenanthousetoronto.ca/get-help/
- 211 listing: https://211central.ca/record/75496026/

### Horizons for Youth
- Emergency shelter / short-term housing for **young men and women ages 16–24** who are homeless or under-housed.
- **Open 24/7**
- Address: **422 Gilbert Ave, Toronto, ON M6E 4X3**
- Main phone: **416-781-9898**
- Application process: **Walk-in** or **call for referral via City central intake 416-338-4766 / 1-877-338-3398 / 311**
- 211 listing: https://211central.ca/record/76299153/

### Eva’s Place
- Emergency shelter in North York for youth experiencing homelessness.
- Address: **360 Lesmill Rd, Toronto, ON M3B 2T5**
- Intake phone: **416-338-4766 (City of Toronto Intake)**
- Page: https://www.evas.ca/where-we-are/

## Suggested qualifier script
"I’m looking for a **youth shelter in Toronto tonight** for someone age **[insert age]**. Do you have a bed available now, or can you direct us to the fastest intake option tonight?"

## Key qualifier questions
- What is the youth’s **age**? (many youth shelters serve **16–24**)
- Are they **safe right now**?
- Can they **travel** to a shelter, and from where?
- Do they need a **specific type of support** tonight? (e.g. trafficking survivor support, mental health, substance use, accessibility)
- Do they have **ID**? If not, ask whether lack of ID blocks intake.

## Notes for handoff
- Start with **central intake** because availability can change quickly.
- If one site is full, ask staff for the **next available youth bed tonight**.
- If calling on behalf of the youth, confirm whether the youth needs to be present on the call or for intake.
